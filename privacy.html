Privacy Policy

How your data is used

All calculations happen locally in your browser. Your debt information is never automatically sent anywhere — we don't store it on a server, and we don't sell it.

When you share a plan, your debt information is encoded in the URL fragment (the part after the #). Fragments are never sent to servers by your browser, so the data stays between you and whoever you share the link with.

If you choose to save your plan by email, your email address and plan link are submitted to Formspree and we use them to send you your plan. See the Waitlist section below for more detail.

Waitlist

If you choose to join the waitlist, your email address and a link to your saved plan are submitted to Formspree and stored on their servers. We use this only to contact you about new features and to send you back your plan link. We will never sell your email or use it for anything else.

No analytics or tracking

This app does not use Google Analytics, cookies, or any third-party tracking. We have no way of knowing who uses it or what they enter.
